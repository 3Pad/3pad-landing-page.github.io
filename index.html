What Is 3Pad?

We are a Web3-based platform that provides landing pages for users. Our platform is designed to give content creators an easy-to-use framework for building and managing a single webpage or landing page. With our platform, content creators can own, control, and engage with their audience in a way that is secure, censor resistant and transparent.

Why Should I Use 3Pad?

With 3Pad, you can effortlessly transition to web3 and enhance your landing experience. Keep your audience engaged and connected by seamlessly linking or embedding your content directly to them on one page. Also keep your users engaed with real time chat, live video or audio all from one page. Let us support you in taking the initial step towards this new and innovative way of sharing with your supporters.

What Is web3?

Web3 refers to the next generation of the internet, which is decentralized and focused on user privacy and security. Unlike the current web, which is centralized and controlled by a few large companies, web3 is powered by decentralized technologies like blockchain and IPFS, and gives users greater control over their data and online identity. The aim of web3 is to create a more equitable, open, and trustworthy internet that works for everyone, not just a few powerful corporations.

Do I have to know code?

No. Users do not need to know how to code in order to create a landing page using our platform. The platform provides a user-friendly interface that allows users to input text, images, and links without needing any coding knowledge.

This means that users can easily create their own landing pages without having to rely on developers or technical experts. They can simply input the content they want to display, such as text and images, and arrange it in a way that suits their needs. Additionally, they can include links to other pages or websites, which will allow their visitors to navigate to other parts of their online presence.

In this way, our platform empowers users to create beautiful, functional landing pages without having to learn how to code. Whether they are creating a personal blog, a professional portfolio, or a business website, they can easily create a landing page that suits their needs and reflects their style.

What is content embedding?

Content embedding and iframes are common techniques used to include content from one website into another website. An iframe is an HTML element that allows you to embed one HTML document within another HTML document, essentially displaying the content of one website within another website. This technique is commonly used to embed videos, maps, and other types of content into a landing page.

When a user visits a landing page with an iframe, their browser requests the content from the source website and displays it within the iframe on the landing page. This allows the landing page to display a range of different content without having to host it all on the same server.

Content embedding and iframes can be useful for a number of different purposes. For example, if you want to include a video or map on your landing page, you can use an iframe to embed that content directly from the source website. This allows you to display dynamic and interactive content without having to write the code yourself.

How Do I get Started?

To get started with our website/app, you will need to sign up using a Web-3 wallet. 

Please note that the site is currently invite-only and in beta, so you may need to request an invitation in order to sign up. You can do this by clicking the connect wallet button. 

You will be prompted to enter your email address and any additional information that we may need in order to process your request. Once you have received an invitation and completed the sign-up process, you may need to purchase an NFT in order to gain access to the Service. 

You can do this by visiting the NFT marketplace and purchasing an NFT that is compatible with our platform or directly from our site on the NFT verification page. 

Once you have an NFT, you can use it to unlock the Service and start using it to share your content with your supporters. You can customize your profile, post updates, and interact with your audience using the features and tools provided by the Service.

What is a NFT?

Non-fungible token.

Non-fungible essentially says that anything is unique and cannot be changed with another object. For instance, a bitcoin may be exchanged for another bitcoin to create the exact identical object.

No files are uploaded to our website/app, so there is no gathering of data in that manner. You own the data and have control over it. You can request access to or deletion of your data from our servers at any time, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
